## What is Duix Avatar?

Duix Avatar is a free and open-source AI avatar project developed by [Duix.com](https://duix.com) that enables you to create ultra-realistic digital humans from real-person video data. This revolutionary technology democratizes digital human creation, making it accessible to everyone with a computer.

Seven years of innovation have led to a breakthrough approach: instead of costly 3D digital human production, Duix Avatar uses AI-generated technology to create ultra-realistic digital humans at a fraction of the cost - reducing production expenses from hundreds of thousands of dollars to just $1,000.

## Key Capabilities

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Appearance and Voice Cloning">
    Using advanced AI algorithms, Duix Avatar captures human facial features with high precision, including facial features, contours, and subtle characteristics. The voice cloning technology reproduces the unique qualities of your voice with support for various voice parameters.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Text and Voice-Driven Virtual Avatars">
    Drive your digital avatar using either text or voice input. Natural language processing converts text to natural, fluent speech while voice input allows avatars to perform corresponding actions and facial expressions based on rhythm and intonation.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Efficient Video Synthesis">
    Industry-leading synchronization technology ensures perfect alignment between digital human video images and sound, achieving natural and smooth lip-syncing with intelligent audio-video optimization.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Docker-Based Deployment">
    Rapid deployment using Docker containers with support for NVIDIA GPU acceleration. Three core services handle ASR (Automatic Speech Recognition), TTS (Text-to-Speech), and video generation.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Technology Stack

Duix Avatar leverages cutting-edge AI technologies:

- **Voice Cloning**: Advanced artificial intelligence generates similar or identical voices based on given voice samples, covering context, intonation, and speed
- **Automatic Speech Recognition (ASR)**: Converts human speech into computer-readable text format using [FunASR](https://github.com/alibaba-damo-academy/FunASR)
- **Text-to-Speech (TTS)**: Based on [Fish Speech](https://github.com/fishaudio/fish-speech) for natural voice synthesis
- **Computer Vision**: Processes visual elements including facial recognition and lip movement analysis to ensure avatar lip movements match voice and text content
